Popure

Popure is an Emotion based Smart Music Player which works on the emotions of a user using facial recognitiontechniques as well as voice commands. Facial data is captured by employing a camera. The emotion module makes use of deep learning techniques to perceive the exact emotional state relative to that expression, hence leading to higher accuracy. It would require permission to access camera from a user’s device or a picture from his/her device gallery. After the emotions are classified ,the app will suggest a playlist to the user pursuant to the emotion detected by it. Also the system has some basic features like Play and Pause audio, Next and Previous tracks etc. It often consists of certain prebuilt playlists of popular songs in different languages. Keeping in mind that people of different age group have different taste in music, Popure has age detection technology using which it would prioritize playlist of songs depending upon user’s age. More often the app involves podcasts of session from counselor. However, the sessions are just to give a synopsis and not prescribed as a therapy. The elite concept of this project is to automatically play songs based on the emotions of the user. It aims to provide user-preferred music with respect to the emotions detected.
